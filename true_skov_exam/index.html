    • Welcome to True Skov

    A peaceful forest offering a retreat into nature located in Brabrand. It’s a haven for explorers, birdwatchers, and anyone seeking tranquility. Discover some hidden spots, the beauty of True Skov and connect with nature in a new way.

    Plants and trees

    Plants and trees

    True Skov is home to a rich variety of plants, from towering trees to delicate ground cover, each playing a vital role in the forest ecosystem.

    As you explore it, you'll encounter a diverse range of native species that thrive in this tranquil environment, adding to the forest's natural beauty

    Mushrooms

    Mushrooms

    True Skov is also home to a variety of fascinating mushroom species, especially in the autumn months.

    The forest's diverse habitats provide ideal conditions for both edible and unique fungi, making it a perfect spot for mushroom enthusiasts to explore.

    Fun Fact

    The Chanterelle mushroom is commonly found in the forests of Denmark, including True Skov Brabrand.

    As you explore it, you'll encounter a diverse range of native species that thrive in this tranquil environment, adding to the forest's natural beauty

    Birds

    Birds

    True Skov Brabrand is home to a variety of bird species, making it a great spot for birdwatching. From vibrant songbirds to elusive owls, the forest offers a diverse birdlife experience throughout the year.

    Cows

    Cows

    True Skov is also home to charming Highland cows and Jutland horses, which roam the forest’s peaceful landscapes, adding to its natural beauty. These majestic animals contribute to the unique character and rural atmosphere of the forest.
