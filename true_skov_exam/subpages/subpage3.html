    • Welcome to True Skov

    A peaceful forest offering a retreat into nature located in Brabrand. It’s a haven for explorers, birdwatchers, and anyone seeking tranquility. Discover some hidden spots, the beauty of True Skov and connect with nature in a new way.

    A cute little wooden bridge in True Skov leads to a designated grazing area, where the Highland cows peacefully roam, completing the serene countryside atmosphere.
    A long wooden bridge in True Skov stretches over the water, with benches along the way, offering a peaceful spot to sit and take in the scenic views of the forest and surrounding nature.
    The long wooden bridge in True Skov is a perfect picture spot, offering stunning views of the forest and water, making it an ideal place to capture the beauty of nature.
