    • Welcome to True Skov

    A peaceful forest offering a retreat into nature located in Brabrand. It’s a haven for explorers, birdwatchers, and anyone seeking tranquility. Discover some hidden spots, the beauty of True Skov and connect with nature in a new way.

    True Skov offers cozy shelters nestled within the forest, providing a perfect spot for visitors to rest and immerse themselves in the natural surroundings.
    Next to the shelters in True Skov, you'll find designated bonfire spots, perfect for gathering around the warmth of the fire while enjoying the tranquil forest atmosphere.
    In the shelter area next to the lake, you can spot cute frogs hopping around, adding to the charm and peaceful ambiance of True Skov.
