    • Welcome to True Skov

    A peaceful forest offering a retreat into nature located in Brabrand. It’s a haven for explorers, birdwatchers, and anyone seeking tranquility. Discover some hidden spots, the beauty of True Skov and connect with nature in a new way.

    True Skov is dotted with a few small lakes, offering quiet spots to relax and enjoy the peaceful beauty of nature.
    True Skov is home to graceful swans, gliding across the tranquil lakes and adding to the serene atmosphere of the forest.
    Highland cows can often be seen walking gracefully around the lakes, grazing on the meadow's lush grass, adding a rustic charm to the peaceful landscape of True Skov
